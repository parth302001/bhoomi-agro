About Us

At Bhoomi, we believe that great food begins with great ingredients. Since our establishment in 1977 by Prakash Shah, we have been delivering high-quality pulses, edible oils, and sugars to customers across India.

Our dedication to unprocessed, chemical-free, and authentic food has earned us the trust of families and businesses alike.
